About Me

Jessa Fayer: A Symphony of Technology and Art

Welcome to the world of Jessa Fayer, where technology meets creativity, and every project is a step towards making the world more intuitive and delightful. At 20, nestled in the vibrant heart of Washington, DC, I embody the spirit of innovation nurtured by my Ukrainian heritage and a passion for empowering students through technology.

Empowering Innovation, Enhancing Experiences

As a senior at the University of Maryland, majoring in Information Science with a concentration in business and computer science, I've dedicated my college career to developing solutions that enhance student life. From co-founding MindGrasp, an AI-driven educational platform, to pioneering StressNYC, a project aimed at improving time management and reducing stress for students, my journey is about creating impactful technology with heart.

My Toolbox

  • UX Design: From user research to user-centered design, I craft experiences that resonate.
  • Data Design: I turn data into insights and actions with a comprehensive skill set in data modeling and visualization.
  • Computer Development: Whether it's full-stack development or RESTful APIs, I build with precision and creativity.

Projects

Spotlight Projects

Passion Project - Stressnyc

The project aims to address the time management challenges faced by students at the University of Maryland, a top-tier institution known for its academic rigor. Beyond their academic responsibilities, students are involved in various extracurricular activities, creating a need for effective tools to navigate their demanding schedules.

Our prototype was designed with the key requirements in mind, and the goal of our product. This design allows users to receive feedback from the AI and Apple Watch which tracks their heart rate at all times.

Currently, I am actively working on the prototype, ensuring it meets the needs of our target users and delivers a seamless experience.

Startup - MindGrasp

I am proud to have co-founded MindGrasp, a university-funded startup, playing a pivotal role in its establishment. MindGrasp revolutionizes education through innovative AI technology, instantly creating accurate notes, summaries, flashcards, quizzes, and answering questions from various sources such as documents, PDFs, videos, and more.

  • Co-founded MindGrasp in December 2021.
  • Spearheaded digital marketing and design initiatives, ensuring a seamless user experience.
  • Led advertising campaigns, achieving significant program visibility and engagement.
  • Developed marketing materials, promoting MindGrasp as the world's foremost AI learning assistant.

    • Spotlight Projects

    MindGrasp: A beacon of innovation in education, making learning accessible and engaging through AI.

Passions Beyond Pixels

My curiosity and zeal for life extend beyond the digital realm. From leading roles in indie films to pirouettes in ballet and ballroom dancing, I embrace every form of artistic expression. As a member of the cybersecurity club, I delve into the intricacies of digital protection, while my involvement in robotics and animation clubs reflects my fascination with creating dynamic, innovative stories.

Clubs and Hobbies

Aside from my academic and professional pursuits, I am deeply passionate about film. I have directed and written multiple film projects and have acted in various productions. I am also involved in ballroom dancing, cyber security club, and ballet, which allow me to explore different facets of my creativity and interests.

Furthermore, I have a diverse range of modeling experiences, from commercials to theater productions and live exhibitions, contributing to my versatile skill set and enriching my personal and professional growth.
