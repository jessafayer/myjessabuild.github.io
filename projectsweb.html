Digital Curation Odyssey: The 4-H National Council Archives Project

Embark on a journey through the intricate and groundbreaking world of digital curation with our INST442 FA2022 Group Project. This expedition saw us navigating the vast digital landscape to safeguard the historical treasures of the 4-H National Council archives.

Our adventure was meticulously charted through six critical phases, each a beacon of innovation and digital stewardship:

  • Archival Reconnaissance: Our initial foray involved delving into the archives, identifying and cataloging records with precision and care. A pilgrimage to the CAFe Lab was essential to this endeavor.
  • Lexical Crafting: We sculpted a lexicon of categories and controlled vocabulary, laying the foundation for a unified archival language.
  • Standardization Saga: With insights from the guardians of 4-H history, we refined our classifications, ensuring each artifact's story would be coherently told.
  • Digital Transmutation: Venturing once more into the CAFe Lab, we transformed selected analog relics into digital form, immortalizing their essence for future generations.
  • Strategic Archival Futures: We envisaged the destiny of these digital treasures, plotting a course for their preservation and access in the digital realm.
  • Culmination Chronicle: The final assembly of our digital dossiers and reflections marked the end of our quest, leaving a legacy of knowledge for the digital age.

Database Design for IT Job Market Insights

Our team embarked on creating a comprehensive database aimed at revolutionizing the IT job market, leveraging data from across Europe to offer deep insights into job trends, salaries, and valued skills. This project highlights the challenges and methodologies of database design and modeling, emphasizing the dynamic IT sector's demands.

Project Highlights:

  • Analysis of the "Polish IT Job Postings" dataset, refined to capture current trends
  • Development of nine tables to detail every aspect of job postings
  • Insights into salary trends, job distribution, and sought-after skills in the IT domain

Challenges and Solutions:

Our journey involved navigating through vast datasets, refining and normalizing data to ensure integrity and utility. Collaboration and expert consultations were pivotal in overcoming these challenges.

Future Directions:

With an eye towards global expansion, we aim to enrich our database with more granular data, including company profiles and job seeker resources, to foster a more inclusive and comprehensive IT job marketplace.

Database Design and Insights

This section outlines the SQL views created for the project and showcases the Entity-Relationship Diagram (ERD) for a comprehensive understanding of the database structure.

SQL Views

The following SQL code snippets illustrate the creation of various views within the database, designed to extract meaningful insights regarding IT job postings.

                        USE inst327project;
                        
                        -- Average salary for each job posting
                        CREATE VIEW posting_average_salary AS
                        SELECT 
                            position.position_id, 
                            title.name AS job_title,
                            company_position_title.experience, 
                            company_position_title.remote,
                            (salary.salary_from + salary.salary_to) / 2 AS avg_salary,
                            salary.currency
                        FROM company_position_title
                        JOIN title USING (title_id)
                        JOIN position USING (position_id)
                        LEFT JOIN salary USING (salary_id)
                        ORDER BY avg_salary DESC;
